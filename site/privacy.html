    Privacy Policy

    Effective date: June 9, 2026 · Last updated: June 9, 2026

    The short version

    • Cardugo requests no permissions other than Face ID — and only if you turn it on.
    • It has no network access, so it never sends your data anywhere.
    • No analytics, no trackers, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs.
    • Everything you enter stays on your device, encrypted with AES-256.
    • No accounts, no servers, no databases — nothing to breach.

    1. Who we are

    Cardugo is an iOS app from an independent developer. Questions about this policy? Email 0@hugohu.top.

    2. What Cardugo stores — and where

    Everything you enter is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

    Data Where stored Encrypted
    Card number, CVV, expiry, cardholder name, PIN, notes, routing & account numbers On device only Yes — AES-256-GCM
    Nickname, bank name, last-four digits On device only No — used for list display
    App settings (theme, language, toggles) On device only No — non-sensitive
    Biometric key (if you enable Face ID) Keychain Yes — iOS Keychain
    Analytics, usage data, crash reports Not collected N/A
    Camera, photos, contacts, location, microphone Not accessed N/A

    3. No permissions, no network, no trackers

    The only permission Cardugo can request is Face ID, used solely to unlock your encrypted data on-device — and only if you enable it. It does not ask for the camera, photos, contacts, location, or microphone.

    Cardugo contains no networking code — no URLSession, no web views, no backend, no cloud sync. It cannot send your data anywhere. It also has zero third-party SDKs: no analytics, no crash reporters, no advertising networks. All card-network detection, validation, and bank-name suggestions run on-device from data bundled with the app.

    4. How your data is protected

    Sensitive fields are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being written to storage. The key is derived from your 6-digit PIN using PBKDF2-SHA256 (200,000 iterations). If you enable Face ID, a copy of the key is sealed in the iOS Keychain. If you forget your PIN, your data cannot be recovered — there is no reset path and no backdoor.

    5. Backups you export

    You can export a .cardugo backup file, AES-256 encrypted with a password you choose at export time. Cardugo never uploads backups automatically — export only happens when you trigger it, and you decide where the file is stored.

    6. Apple, children & changes

    When you download from the App Store, Apple may collect data as described in Apple's Privacy Policy; that is handled by Apple, not by us. Cardugo is not directed at children and knowingly collects no data from anyone. If our practices ever change, we will update this page and the date above.

    7. Contact

    Questions? Reach us at 0@hugohu.top.
